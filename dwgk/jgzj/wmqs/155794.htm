我的教学故事---A Pair Of Wings Which Is Invisible

发布日期:2010-11-24 12:00:00   来源:   
 

Zhu yun


　　Most people are always thinking that marks is above everything. Teachers judge the students from their marks. Truly, marks functions cannot be underestimated.

　　But, I think , responsibility ,kindness, selflessness, persistence and other moral characters are more important .Even those students who can not achieve good marks in subjects have one of these morals.what I should do is to help these students find their outstanding qualities which can chage their life into a better one.

　　People always say that children are the gifts from the god .so they do believe every child is an angle . The angles come to this world with a pair of wings which is invisible.

　　While Teachers’ love, trust and encourgement make their wings grow stronger in hesitation and loneliness , flying as far as the wind can take.

　　Being a teacher for five years , I have met all kinds of angles, the angel of restiveness but kind, angle of wickedness but smart ,angle of rebellion but filial ……um…the angle from Korea, sweet and nice.

　　And this is the angle I meet in this new school----angle of persistence.

　　Frankly speaking, he is not qualified in study.He is poor at maths ,poor at chinese, poor at English. Even in other subjects,he is below average

I tell him:

　　The little boy who says "I＇ll try"
　　Will climb to the hill-top.
　　The little boy who says "I can＇t".
　　Will at the bottom stop.
　　＇I＇ll try" does great things every day.
　　"I can＇t" gets nothing done.

　　Afer my words, boy says

　　"I＇ll try".

　　From that day,

　　To recite the new words ,he will sit at the desk for half an hour,an hour ,even two hours without a little move.

　　To finish his calss projects , he will practise cutting the broad paper again and again until he made an ugly , wizened flower . when he showed his flower to me, I said ,wow….. it is nice ,steven . it is like a real flower. You really did a good job.”

　　He smiled and said ,thank you ,fiona. if you think I can do that , I can.,

　　At that moment , Steven’s words let me know the power of trust.but do you know ,steven, it is your persistence that keep you work and life going on .

　　I wrote on his book:

　　Nothing in the world can take the place of persistence.
　　Talent will not; nothing is more common than unsuccessful individuals with talent.
　　Genius will not; unrewarded genius is just a proverb.
　　Education will not; the world is full of educated derelicts.

　　Persistence is your wings that lift your up,get closer to your dream. So,Keep walking and don’t stop ,steven.

　　Welll,time limited, I don’t have enough time to let you meet all my angles.what I really want to say is that Life is filled with suffering, but it is also filled with many wonders.

　　To suffer is not enough. We must also be in touch with the wonders .

　　As a teacher ,never,ever judge a student from his marks, or you will miss a lot of things, what you should do is to help them find their wings and make angles’ wings strong enough //to touch the wonders. no matter how much it hurts.
